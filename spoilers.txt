Choices at the start of the game:
	1. Normal gameplay.
	2. Enables the encounter at Sector 7 Exit Beacon.
	3. 50% chance of the encounter at Sector 7 Exit Beacon.

Choices on encounter:
	1: Pay 600 scrap for upgrading both the maximum and current level of Shields and Weapon Control by 3 (Levels 9-11 of both systems will be priced at 120 scrap, unless you have other mods that define their values). Then, fight.
	2: Fight.

After killing enemy crew:
	High scrap and resources, and unlock the ship.

Choices after surrender:
	1. Medium scrap and resources, and unlock the ship.
	2. Continue the fight.

After ship is destroyed:
	High scrap and resources.
